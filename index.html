Welcome to VibesCast! We're a team of four friends with a shared passion for music, weather, and bringing a little extra joy to your daily routine. Our site combines the practical with the whimsical: offering you not just the weather forecast, but also the perfect playlist to match it.

Ever found yourself wishing you had the ideal soundtrack for a rainy day, a sunny afternoon, or a snowy commute? We did too! That's why we created VibesCast. Whether you're gearing up for a jog in the park, bracing for a downpour, or simply looking for tunes to brighten a gloomy day, we've got you covered.

Our goal is to make your daily journey a bit more enjoyable, no matter what the skies have in store. So, go ahead, search for your city, check the forecast, and discover the perfect playlist to complement your weather. Here's to good vibes and great music!
